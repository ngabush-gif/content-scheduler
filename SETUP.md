# ContentCreator Hub - Team Setup Guide

## How to Add Team Members (Manual Manus Collaborators)

Your app is **private** and only accessible to authorized users. You control access through Manus.

### Step 1: Add Collaborator in Manus
1. Open Manus Management UI (the dashboard where you manage your app)
2. Go to **Settings** → **Collaborators** (or **Team** section)
3. Click **"Add Collaborator"** or **"Invite Team Member"**
4. Enter your team member's **email address**
5. Click **Send Invite**

### Step 2: Team Member Accepts Invite
1. Team member receives email invite from Manus
2. They click the link in the email
3. They accept the collaboration invite
4. They now have access to your app

### Step 3: Team Member Logs In
1. They go to your app URL: `https://zaygao8.manus.space` (or your custom domain)
2. They click **"Sign In"**
3. They authenticate with Manus OAuth (email/password or SSO)
4. They're logged in and can start creating content

### What They Can Do
- ✅ Create content with AI Generator
- ✅ Generate images
- ✅ See only their own content (not others')
- ✅ Publish to Facebook, Instagram, TikTok
- ✅ Access all features independently

### What They CANNOT Do
- ❌ See other team members' content
- ❌ Manage team members (only you can)
- ❌ Change app settings

## How to Remove Team Member Access

### Step 1: Go to Collaborators in Manus
1. Open Manus Management UI
2. Go to **Settings** → **Collaborators**
3. Find the team member you want to remove

### Step 2: Remove Access
1. Click **"Remove"** or **"Revoke Access"** next to their name
2. Confirm the action
3. They **immediately lose access** to your app

---

## Important Notes

- **Each team member has their own login** - they use their own email and password
- **Each team member sees only their own content** - complete privacy/isolation
- **All team members are admins** - they can create and publish independently
- **No approval needed** - they can publish directly without your review
- **Easy to remove** - revoke access anytime from Manus settings

## Troubleshooting

**Team member says "You don't have permission to view this page"**
- They haven't been added as a collaborator yet
- Go to Manus Settings → Collaborators and add them
- They need to accept the invite email

**Team member can't find the app**
- Make sure they're logged in with the correct email
- Send them your app URL directly
- Check they accepted the collaboration invite

**Team member was removed but still has access**
- Wait a few minutes for the change to sync
- They may need to logout and login again
- Clear browser cache if needed
